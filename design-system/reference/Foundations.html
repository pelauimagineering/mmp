MMP · Design System v0.1

Friend-group sport, kept honest.

MMP is the umbrella over a poker dealer and a golf scoring app shared by the same group of friends since 2008. The design system lets the two surfaces feel like one place, while letting each one feel right at the table or on the tee.

Two themes, one spine

Every component is theme-aware. Apply .theme-felt on the poker app, .theme-links on golf and marketing. Tokens swap; type, spacing, radii, and component shapes don’t.

Links

Light. Editorial. Fairway green on cream parchment, with engraved-plaque gold for the cup. Used by the golf app and the MMP homepage.

bg #F6F1E7 fairway #2F5D3A gold #B08D3A clay #B54A2A ink #14151A

Felt

Dark. Casino-room. Built on the live poker-dealer tokens so we don’t fight the existing app. Trophy gold replaces the lone-green-everywhere look.

Type

Motion

Most state changes use --dur (220 ms) on --ease-out. Card flips and tee-time reveals can use --ease-spring for personality. Avoid bouncy easing on numbers — scores should feel certain.

Logo & brotherhood mark
